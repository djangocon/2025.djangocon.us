Welcome to DjangoCon US 2025 Online


We are so happy to be hosting you online. We will be hosting discussions on Slack and are looking forward to chatting with you there while watching the conference.

We have two stages, Wolf Point and Sauganash, where you can watch speakers while you ask questions in our Slack.

Looking for something?

  • Slack

    This is where we will be chatting. Come ask the speakers questions. Here you can also look at job opportunities, find the help desk, announcements updating speaker times, and hang out with us

  • Sponsors

    We are so lucky to have such amazing support from the community. Come see what our community is up to!

  • Help!

    Have general question about Venueless? Need help accessing Slack? General technical issues? Looking for information about accessibility? This is the place.

  • Code of Conduct

    DjangoCon US is dedicated to providing a fun harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion. If you need help or more information about our Code Of Conduct, access it on this page.
